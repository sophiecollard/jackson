Grover Jackson Randy Rhoads Custom

1990-1994

Early 90s Japanese Market Exclusive MOP Inlays

Overview

A high-end Japanese-made model released for the domestic market only. While the first ones came with 22 frets, some time around 1991 Jackson updated this model to 24 frets, making it (to my knowledge) the first 24-frets Rhoads catalogue model.

Specs

Construction: Neck-through body

Scale: 25.5" (648 mm)

Number of frets: As per 1991 catalogue: 22

Number of frets: As per 1993 catalogue: 24

Body: Yellow Poplar

Neck: Quartersawn Maple

Fingerboard: Ebony

Inlays: MOP Sharkfins

Fret Wire: Rockwell C6/Brinell 171

Fret Size: .118 x .114 x .051 x .037

Pickups: As per 1991 catalogue: J-50N (neck), J-50B (bridge)

Pickups: As per 1993 catalogue: J-50BC (neck), J-90C (bridge)

Controls: 2x Volume, 1x Tone, 3-Way Toggle Switch

Tuners: Grover Jackson SG38-06

Bridge: Jackson-branded Schaller double locking tremolo

Bridge Mount: Non-recessed with full stretch cavity

Finishes: As per 1991 catalogue: Pearl Gold, Metallic Black, Pearl Blue White

Finishes: As per 1993 catalogue: Metallic Black, Pearl Blue White, Aluminium, Dark Blue

Price: As per 1991 catalogue: ¥195,000 (with hard case)

Price: As per 1993 catalogue: ¥200,000 (with hard case)
